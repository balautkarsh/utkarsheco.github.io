About

I am Utkarsh Kumar, currently pursuing PhD at Indian institute of Technology Kanpur (IIT Kanpur) from Department of Economic Sciences under the supervision of Dr. Wasim Ahmad . I am expecting to get my degree by mid 2024.

In my doctoral research titled "Essays in Macroeconomic Forecasts, I leverage Bayesian and classical regression techniques for forecasting critical macroeconomic variables like Exchange Rates, Inflation and Industrial Prodcution. My thesis work explores a diverse range of methodologies, including shrinkage priors, nonlinear models, variable selection, and non-parametric approaches, to enhance prediction accuracy and provide valuable insights for policymakers and economic stakeholders.
